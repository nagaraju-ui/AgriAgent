# PMFBY crop insurance
The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) provides insurance cover and financial
support to farmers in the event of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests and
diseases. Farmer premium is low (around 2 percent for kharif food and oilseed crops,
1.5 percent for rabi, and 5 percent for commercial and horticultural crops).

# PM-KISAN income support
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi gives eligible landholding farmer families income
support of Rs 6000 per year in three equal instalments.

# Soil Health Card
The Soil Health Card scheme issues cards with crop-wise nutrient recommendations,
helping farmers apply the right fertiliser dose and improve yield.

# Kisan Credit Card
The Kisan Credit Card provides short-term credit for cultivation expenses at a
subsidised interest rate, reducing dependence on informal lenders.
